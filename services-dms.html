94% of all games spending is digital.

The digital market has evolved considerably as historical platform dominance is disrupted by regulatory pressure, and savvy gamers demand more ‘retail’ choice.

Game makers have never been more under pressure to deliver commercial results. Visibility in a cluttered marketplace is challenging, and dominant platforms do little to drive success beyond the wishes of an algorithm.

For nearly 2 decades Green Man Gaming has been at the forefront of digital retail. We have learned a lot. 2 years ago we decided to offer this expertise to a handful of partners in the form of a managed service.

Introducing DMS:
A Comprehensive Managed Service

DMS is a comprehensive managed service, designed to protect your IP and improve your revenue position by maximizing global sales and margin and enhancing overall game visibility.

Why you need IT?

  • Our Service delivers on average an incremental 20% of revenue on top of your Steam Sales.
  • We offer a fully integrated API giving you full transparency.
  • Amplifying your reach with additional visibility to over 100 million engaged gamers across 150 stores globally.
  • We provide full key management which includes Security, pricing, region locks asset management, and audit.
  • Tailored marketing and retail checks.
  • Reporting – Royalty reports from all stores in one place.
  • 15+ years of digital retail experience, on call for you.
  • Free up your time – You can focus on your game; we will handle the execution.
