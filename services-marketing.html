services – Marketing

As a publisher and a retailer, we understand the importance marketing plays to turn your passion project into commercial success.

Our team works with you to deliver highly effective campaigns that maximise your marketing $$’s and deliver outstanding reach and ROI.

Awareness is great, but conversion is better, and we have the capability and experience to do both, in-house.

Our success is built on the backbone of years’ of 1st party data, giving us a unique insight of the worldwide gaming audience.

Our tactics.

We use Millions of 1st party data points to create gaming audiences who have wishlisted or purchased similar games to yours. This ‘look a like’ data is plugged in behind the campaign creatives on the relevant performance channels to drive wishlist & pre-purchase awareness & conversions.

Everything is targeted and highly cost effective.

We can also provide accurate attribution tracking unlike anyone else to show which channel performed best and full post campaign analysis that you can use for future campaigns.

All of our campaigns are bespoke, we have experts in the following disciplines:

SEARCH
  • Data driven keyword analysis driving demand and visibility across Google Performance Max, Bing, Yahoo Japan, Naver etc.
  • Programmatic display & retargeting
SOCIAL
  • Expertise on all platforms: Meta, X, Reddit, YT, Tik Tok & Naver, Discord (panel)
Email
  • 1.4M opted in database
  • Custom templates to showcase your game
Influencers
  • We work with influencers across all platforms and all genres
  • Industry leading tools to discover, track and measure
Affiliates
  • A mature and established network of Global partners
  • Industry leading conversion (11.7%)
Steam algorithms
  • Utilising machine learning and data analytics to personalise recommendations and enhance user experiences
