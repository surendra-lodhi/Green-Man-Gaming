Publishing – publishers with retail in our blood.

BAFTA winning boutique Publishing business

Established in 2018, Green Man Gaming Publishing specialises in working with Indie game creators from around the world.

Our team love Indie games and are ready to sell their soul to make your game a success. Our people come from a variety of backgrounds in production, marketing, and management. We can scale up or down depending on what you need.

As part of the Green Man Gaming group, we provide our partners access to finishing finance, marketing expertise, global distribution, access to technology partners and of course our store. We offer flexible contracts, and we won’t ever try and take control of your IP.

Not everyone wants or needs a Publisher, but if issues arise it’s always better to have great support behind you. We see over 1500 games a year; we’d love to see yours.

Your Game. Our Expertise. Everyone’s success.

We only take on a few projects a year, by design. Our partners aren’t just a line in a yearly publishing slate, they’re our priority. Here’s what we bring to the table when we mobilise the team.

  • Funding assistance:We can provide full or partial funding to help finish your journey.
  • Asset Creation: Our in-house designers are experienced in making assets that get noticed, freeing you up to focus on the game and not the marketing materials.
  • Production support: Milestone planning to content direction, platform support and submissions. We source the resources to support your production.
  • Distribution:Our global network includes every digital and physical channel for PC and console, we will be your single point to access worldwide distribution.
  • Product Management: From platform strategy to price, our experienced team will guide you to launch as well as extending the product life cycle.
  • Porting: With our network of trusted porting partners, we can provide reliable and cost-effective support to hit all platforms.
  • Localisation and Quality Assurance: We can facilitate all language localisation as well as give external feedback on your title, from in-house play testing to in-depth QA options.
  • Global reach: With a global presence and dedicated teams in high growth markets, including LATAM and China, we’ll tailor plans to achieve the maximum impact in every region.
  • Go-to-Market Planning: Delivering results-driven campaigns that generate hype and create sales, across brand management, partnerships, PR, social, community and influencer management, media buying and more.
  • Access to B2B Partners: We partner with some of the biggest names in tech, putting you shoulder to shoulder with trusted brands.
